Overview

A wooden shack up at the most treacherous heights of the The Redridge Mountains. It's completely isolated from the on-goings of the world around it. Your best bet for company are the local wildlife, any friends you brought with you and the dark. However, a small settlement does live near the base of the mountain which poses a risk of being seen by locals anytime you exit the quickest route down from the Shack.

  • Ipos routinely casts Plant Growth to farm corn and potatoes; alongside hunting trips further down The Redridge Mountains.
  • The The Winter Spirit lingers up atop The Redridge Mountains, an old habit cemented from past days feasting on the despair and starvation of Ashkovian and Verenar soldiers during the Great Yorokkan war.
  • Winds are strong enough to occassionaly tear planks from the Shack's roof.
  • Ipos out on a hunt struggling and finally finding a distant dead carcass of a deer. Ipos approaches only for it to disappear in a gust of wind the moment. Ipos gets close. She hears it snickering, weezing on the wind.
